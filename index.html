The Solution

The Dual-Tier Model

EcoSort splits the experience into two layers: a premium in-home device for resident ease, and a building-level hub for operational efficiency and scalable infrastructure.

The Lite Device

Built to belong in the modern kitchen.

EcoSort Pod Lite is compact, refined, and intentionally quiet in both form and function. With its sage-green body and premium material detailing, it feels more like an appliance than a bin.

Minimal footprint for apartment countertops or utility corners.
